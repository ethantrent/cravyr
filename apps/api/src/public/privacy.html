Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Cravyr ("we," "our," or "the app") is a restaurant discovery app. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: Email address, display name, and authentication provider (Google or Apple). If you sign in with Apple, we only receive the name you choose to share.
  • Location data: Your approximate location (latitude and longitude) when you use the Discover screen. We request "While Using" permission only — never background location.
  • Usage data: Swipe actions (left/right/superlike), saved restaurants, and cuisine/price preferences you set in the app.
  • Device tokens: An Expo push notification token so we can send you optional notifications.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Show nearby restaurants ranked by your preferences and location.
  • Remember your saved restaurants ("Tonight's Picks").
  • Send push notifications you have opted into (e.g., daily picks reminders).
  • Improve recommendations over time based on your swipe history.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase (database and authentication) — stores your account and app data in a PostgreSQL database with row-level security. Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Google Places API — provides restaurant information (name, address, photos, hours, ratings). Your location is sent to Google to retrieve nearby restaurants. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Expo Push Notifications — delivers push notifications to your device. Expo Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase's managed PostgreSQL infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit (TLS). Restaurant photos are served directly from Google's CDN — we do not store photo files. Access to your personal data is protected by row-level security policies so that only you can read your own swipes, saves, and preferences.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account (Settings → Delete Account), all personal data — including swipes, saves, preferences, and push tokens — is permanently removed via cascading deletes. Cached restaurant data (which is not personal) may persist.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: View your saved data inside the app (saved restaurants, preferences).
  • Deletion: Delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account.
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications in your device settings or revoke location permission.

7. Children's Privacy

Cravyr is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Questions? Reach us at privacy@cravyr.app.
