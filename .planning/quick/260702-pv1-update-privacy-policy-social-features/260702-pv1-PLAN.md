# Quick Task: Update privacy policy + App Privacy declarations for social features

## Why

Commits fd2a683 and de2eca5 (June 28) shipped friends/connections, group matches, and
travel mode. The privacy policy (last updated April 24) and the runbook's App Privacy
declaration checklist predate these features. Submitting to the App Store with an
inaccurate privacy policy / App Privacy label is a rejection and compliance risk.

## What the new features collect (verified against code)

- `connections` table: mutual friend links between user IDs (supabase/migrations/20260720000000_connections.sql)
- `connection_codes` table: random invite codes tied to a user ID, expire after 15 minutes
- `GET /api/v1/connections` exposes each friend's display name (user_metadata.full_name
  or email prefix fallback) to connected users (apps/api/src/routes/connections.ts:58)
- `get_group_matches` RPC (SECURITY DEFINER): reveals the intersection of saved
  restaurants among the user and selected friends — friends see mutually-saved
  restaurants only, never raw swipe history, skips, or location
- `DELETE /api/v1/connections/:friendId` lets users remove a friend
- All rows cascade-delete with the auth.users row (ON DELETE CASCADE)
